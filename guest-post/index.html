Want to write for MBF? Awesome! If you have some kick-ass content that our audience will enjoy, we'd love to publish it.

Here are a few guidelines to follow:

  1. Read our past posts. Notice how in-depth and actionable they are? These are the kinds of articles we're looking for. We're not into mind-numbing blocks of text or posts shorter than 500 words.
  2. Stick to our topic. We only write about marketing for startup companies.
  3. Keep your links in check. Link to anything useful and relevant, but please avoid any affiliate or self-promotional links.
  4. Include a quick bio. Make it 2-3 sentences max and feel free to add links to your site or social media.
  5. Send your post in HTML. The less formatting we have to do, the sooner your article will go live.
  6. Submit via email. When you're ready to send your article, email it (as an attachment) to misha [at] mishaabasov.com. We'll respond as soon as we can.

That's it. We hope to see a submission from you soon!
