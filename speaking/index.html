Mike Abasov is the guy behind Marketing Before Funding. You can find out more about him on our About page.

Not surprisingly, Mike speaks about marketing for startups: from branding and community building, to networking and pitching to investors. He has been speaking at universities, workshops, and conferences since 2008.

His presentations are very relaxed and down-to-earth, and he always adds humour with unexpected analogies and examples to keep people entertained. His goal is to communicate everything in simple words while maintaining the highest quality of content.
